About Genesis

Join us for the "GDG Genesis Hackathon 2025," an exhilarating journey exclusively crafted for the innovative minds of 1st-year students at SATI College! Kickstart your tech adventure with our multi-stage challenge:

  • Registration: (15 Oct - 26 OCT, 2025) - All the First Year Student of SATI, Vidisha
  • 1st Stage: Ideation Round (Date TBD, Online) - Brainstorm and submit your groundbreaking ideas.
  • 2nd Stage: Mentoring cum Evaluation Round (Date TBD, Online) - Collaborate with experienced mentors to refine your project.
  • Final Stage: Showdown (Date TBD, Offline) - Bring your ideas to life and showcase them to win exciting prizes!

Note: Only members who complete the RSVP will be eligible for participation.

This hackathon is designed to introduce first-year students to project-based problem-solving, collaborative development, and real-world technical challenges. It will encourage creativity, teamwork, and early engagement with software development tools and concepts. This hackathon aims to provide first-year students with a practical learning opportunity that blends creativity with coding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. Who need to RSVP, Only Team Leader or All Team Members?

All participants, including team leaders and team members, must individually scan the QR code on the poster and RSVP for the event.

Q2. What happens after I complete the RSVP?

After submitting the RSVP form, you will receive a confirmation email on your registered email ID. Later you will be receiving the PPT Submission form at this registered email.

Q3. Is there any session before the hackathon?

Yes. We will be hosting a short Kick-off and Doubt-Clearing Session to explain event details and answer queries. The session will be held on 26th October, and the exact time will be decided based on the majority selection from the poll.

Q4. How will we submit our idea?

After Kick-off Session, a Google Form link will be sent to all registered participants via email. This form will be used to enter your team details and upload your Idea PPT.

Q5. Where can I find the submission guidelines?

All submission guidelines will be clearly mentioned in the Google Form and will also be explained during the Kick-off Session.

Q6. Does RSVPing confirm my registration?

No Your registration will be confirmed only after you submit your Idea PPT.

Q7. How do I RSVP?

Simply scan the QR code on the poster and click/touch the blue RSVP button. It takes less than a minute.

Q8. How many members can I have in a team?

You can 2 to 4 members in a team. No Solo participation allowed and Each member should be from 1st year.

Q9. What ideas should I choose?

We promote Open Innovation, so you're free to choose any idea. Be it a problem you face, an improvement to an existing solution, something out-of-the-box, something people never thought of or something you wish existed to solve your problem. Sky is the limit, be creative & unique.

Q10. What should I do now?

By now, you should have your team and idea ready. Next, start preparing your Idea PPT. More details about the PPT format and submission will be shared during the Info Session. Once your idea is approved, you can start working on your prototype.
